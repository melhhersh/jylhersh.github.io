Sweetheart Pet Services offers a variety of training options at reasonable rates.

Utilizing both Fear-Free training and TTouch techniques the focus is specific to the needs of the owner / shelter / rescue group and the animals.

  • Private Session with the Trainer (Zoom session or In Person session)
  • Semi-Private Sessions (Zoom or In Person)
    • Partner with a friend or family member for a shared lesson
  • Virtual Classes
    • Offered through PAWs Shelter, including free Ask The Trainer Happy Hours
  • Group Classes
    • Offered both privately and through PAWs shelter
  • Pet Loss and Grief Support Group
  • Preparing your Dog for your New Baby (class and private session)
  • TTouch Classes - Any group is invited to host these:
    • Single Session Introduction to Tellington TTouch
    • 4 Hour Tellington TTouch Class
    • One-Day and Two Day Tellington TTouch Seminars
